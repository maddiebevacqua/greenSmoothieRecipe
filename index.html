Green Smoothie Recipe

This is the best green smoothie in the world. It has all of the vitamin A you need in a day and it tastes so dang good. Green smoothies are usually not super great but this one is an exception. This recipe will serve two but it is so delicious that you will only want to serve yourself.

Ingredients

  • 200g spinach
  • 1½ frozen RIPE bananas
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 2 tbsp hemp hearts
  • 2 tbsp goji berries
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed

Directions

  1. If you have not already froze your ripe bananas, freeze your bananas now and make this smoothie tomorrow.
  2. Combine your chia seeds and flaxseeds with ¼ cup coconut water and let sit for 4 minutes or until liquid is absorbed by the seeds (optional).
  3. If you have a vitamix you can add all of your ingredients and blend away because those things can blend rocks. If you do not have a vitamix move on to the next step.
  4. Blend liquids with spinach, hemp hearts, goji berries, chia seeds, and flaxseeds on high until smooth.
  5. Add bananas and blend on high until smooth.
  6. Pour into cup and drink with straw if you do not care about the environment (it tastes better with a straw).
